Sked.io

Schedule planning and sharing site for UMD Students

Sked.io let's registered users plan and share their schedule for the University of Maryland. Course data for the site was gathered by webscraping the UMD Schedule of Classes.

Edit Sidebar

Use quick-add form if you already have a section or course you need to add.

Predictive search

Search for a department by name or abbreviation, then search for a course by it's name or ID. Selectable suggestions appear as the user types

Section selection

After searching for or viewing a course, view available sections grouped by professor. Each section row containes information on meeting times and locations as well as a schedule snapshot. Click a section row to toggle.

Dynamic display

The weekly calendar algorithm can display overlapping times with ease without affecting the rest of the day

Friendships

View a friend's schedule, then overlap them with yours to compare availability
